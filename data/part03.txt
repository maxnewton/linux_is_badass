They didn't have any of those, newfangled graphical operating systems in the old days! DOS will do just fine!

Oh-ho! What's this? Out of the corner of your eye you spy a Commander Keen install floppy in the box. Looks like this is your lucky day! You slip the floppy in the disk drive, run the installer, and spend the rest of the afternoon enjoying the best that early 1990's gaming could offer.

Around 6pm you realize that you would, in fact, like to have a modern web browser.
