Just then, you are struck by a brilliant thought. "Everyone uses Windows. I'll just install that."

Unfortunately the most recent copy of Windows you have in your "box of old computer crap" is 3.1. And, as nostalgic as it might be to relive the early 1990's...you just can't bring yourself to install it. So, to the store it is!

After perusing the aisles of your local computer store - and dodging no less than 37 blue-shirted marauders, assaulting you with a declaration of "Welcome to Nerd Stuff" - you decide on just picking up a copy of Windows 8. The reason why you decide to buy Windows 8 may remain shrouded in mystery until the end of time...though it may have something to do with the fact that you wanted to use Windows...and that was the only version available.
